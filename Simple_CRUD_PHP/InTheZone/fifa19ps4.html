Release Date: 28/09/2018

Do you have what it takes to take your team to the top of the league? Lead your teams to victory on PS4.

EA Sports have returned, offering incredible and unrivalled ways to play across a variety of game modes and new leagues. Enjoy the pinnacle of football with the new and improved features!

UEFA CHAMPIONS LEAGUE/EUROPA LEAGUE/SUPER CUP

The UEFA Champions League, Europa League, and Super Cup have and are offering up a brand-new commentary team of Derek Rae and Lee Dixon. You can now experience the world’s greatest football leagues in all elements.

Introducing a brand-new mode, Champions League Mode, you can be in the centre of the drama and excitement of the official UEFA Champions League tournament all the way from group stages to the final match.

To add even more realism into the gameplay, there are official broadcast elements included throughout game play. These include match balls, kit badges and the iconic trophies from the UEFA Champions League, Europa League, and Super Cup.

THE JOURNEY: CHAMPIONS

The Journey: Champions will follow three football heroes, each with their own fascinating stories. Follow the respective and challenging journeys of Alex Hunter, Danny Williams, and Kim Hunter as they reach some of the most significant moments of their careers. Each of their stories intertwine and enable you to switch between the three characters at any time.

You’ll cross paths with some of the of the world’s most prestigious footballers, including Neymar Jr., Alex Morgan, Paulo Dybala, Kevin De Bruyne, and many more.

After Kim had her big break into the US Women’s National Team in The Journey: Hunter Returns, she steps into the lead role in this trilogy’s final chapter. Play as Kim as she flourishes in her personal journey to represent the US at the Women’s World Cup.

EVEN MORE NEW MODES….

But that’s not all that’s coming to FIFA 19 on PS4. There are even more new modes and features such as new kick-off experience, career mode, pro-cubs and skill games.
